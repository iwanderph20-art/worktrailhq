Billing compliance built for small labs.

Lab Compliance Pro validates your CPT/ICD-10 billing claims against your state's Medicare Administrative Contractor (MAC) Local Coverage Determinations — in real time, with actionable remediation advice.

MAC Jurisdiction Engine

Select your state and instantly map to your MAC contractor. Coverage rules load automatically by region.

Real-Time LCD Validation

CPT + ICD-10 pairs checked against active Local Coverage Determinations. Covered, conditional, or denied — with the policy reference cited.

Compliance Health Score

Every validation updates your A–F grade in real time. Know your denial risk before claims go out the door.

No PHI stored or transmitted. BAA-compatible design. Built for covered entities and their billing teams.
