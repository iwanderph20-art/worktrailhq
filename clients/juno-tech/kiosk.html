💋 Clock-In Approval Notifications

Admin receives an email on every clock-in with Approve/Deny buttons. Staff see "Sent for Approval" and can't clock out until approved.

Setup (free): Go to emailjs.com → Email Services → Add Office 365 → sign in with your Microsoft account → Create Template.

Template variables: {{employee_name}}, {{clock_time}}, {{facility}}, {{note}}, {{kiosk_url}}, {{photo_note}}

"To Email" field: set to {{to_email}}

Note: Email is notification-only. Admin clicks the kiosk link in the email to approve or deny from the Records tab.
