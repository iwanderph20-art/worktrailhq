🧬 Lab Compliance Pro
CPT / ICD-10 Claim Validator · Setup Guide

Thanks for your purchase! Lab Compliance Pro is a single file (lab-compliance.html) that runs in any browser — no install, no account, no cloud. It processes billing codes only; no patient data is ever stored or sent.

1 · Open it (10 seconds)

1
Double-click the file.

lab-compliance.html opens in your default browser. That's it — it's ready to use offline.

2
Bookmark or pin it.

Add it to your bookmarks bar (or, on a tablet, "Add to Home Screen") so your billing team can reach it in one tap.

3
Day or night.

Use the 🌙 / ☀️ button (top-right) to pick a light or dark look. Your choice is remembered on that device.

2 · Validate a claim

1
Pick the jurisdiction.

Choose the patient's state / territory on the left. The tool shows the governing Medicare Administrative Contractor (MAC) and its active policy.

2
Enter the codes.

Type or pick CPT procedure codes and ICD-10 diagnosis codes — each becomes a chip. Start typing a code or name and pick from the suggestions.

3
Validate.

Press Validate for an instant COVERED / NOT COVERED / CONDITIONAL verdict with the reason, the LCD/policy reference, and a next step.

3 · Score & audit log

Each validation updates your Compliance Health Score (a live A–F grade for the share of claims that come back covered) and is added to the Audit Log for the session — codes, verdict, and policy reference. Open the Audit Log tab to review or clear it. The log holds billing codes only, never PHI, and clears on page reload.

Use it on a shared device (kiosk)

Great for a billing workstation. Open the file in the browser, pin the tab, and your team validates claims all day. Nothing is uploaded, so it's safe on any office machine. To host it on an internal page or intranet, upload lab-compliance.html to any static host (your web server, Netlify Drop, SharePoint, etc.) and share the link.

Important — this is decision support, not billing advice. Coverage data reflects the LCD/policy versions shown in the app. Payer policies change; always confirm a verdict against your MAC's current published LCD before you rely on it. This tool does not constitute billing, legal, or medical advice.
Privacy. Everything runs locally in your browser. No claim data, codes, or results ever leave the device — there is no server and no account.
