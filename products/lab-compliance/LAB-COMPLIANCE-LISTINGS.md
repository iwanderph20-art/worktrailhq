**Description:**
Lab Compliance Pro is a complete pre-bill compliance checker for a clinical or pathology lab, in
a single file. Your biller enters the **CPT procedure codes** and **ICD-10 diagnosis codes** on a
claim, selects the patient's **state**, and the tool maps that state to its **Medicare
Administrative Contractor (MAC)** and validates the pairing against that contractor's **Local
Coverage Determination (LCD)**.

Every check returns a clear verdict — **COVERED**, **NOT COVERED**, or **CONDITIONAL** — with the
reason, the governing LCD/policy reference, and a plain-language next step. It's the difference
between catching a medical-necessity denial at the desk and eating it 45 days later.

**What it does:**
- **Claim Validator** — paste CPT + ICD-10 codes, pick a state, get an instant coverage verdict with the reason and the LCD reference behind it.
- **All 50 states + DC, PR & USVI** mapped to their MAC (Palmetto, Noridian, Novitas, NGS, WPS, First Coast, CGS) and active policy version.
- **Compliance Health Score** — a live A–F score and gauge tracking the % of your checked claims that are covered, so you can see denial exposure at a glance.
- **Audit Log** — every validation is logged for the session (codes + verdict + policy ref) and can be reviewed or cleared. No PHI, only billing codes.
- **Compliance Guide** — how the score is graded and a table of current MAC policy versions.
- **Day / Night** (light & dark) mode.

**Why buy it:**

- **No PHI, no risk.** It processes billing codes only — no patient data is stored, sent, or logged. HIPAA-ready by design.
- **Works offline, in any browser.** A single file — no install, no accounts, no cloud.
- **Cut denials.** Catch non-covered CPT/ICD-10 pairings and medical-necessity gaps *before* you submit.

**Who it's for:** billing managers and coders at independent clinical labs, pathology groups,
physician office labs (POLs), and small reference labs who want a fast pre-bill sanity check without
a five-figure claim-scrubbing platform.

**Please note — this is a guide, not a full compliance system.** Lab Compliance Pro checks CPT/ICD-10
code coverage against LCD reference data. It does **not** review, create, store, or replace progress
notes, physician/doctor's notes, medical records, or any other clinical documentation required to
support a claim — that stays your responsibility. Coverage data reflects the LCD/policy versions shown
in the app and is a decision-support aid only, not billing, legal, or medical advice; always confirm
against your MAC's current policy. This is a digital tool, not a Canva/PDF template.
