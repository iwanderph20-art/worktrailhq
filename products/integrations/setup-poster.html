012 min

Create your free database

Go to firebase.google.com → Realtime Database → Create (test mode), then copy the URL that ends in firebaseio.com.

Bought Done-for-you Hosting? Skip this step — we handle it for you.

021 min

Set up Worktrail

Open the app and choose Set Up My Kiosk. Enter your business name, logo and color, pick an admin password, then paste your Firebase URL.

031 min

Add your team

In Admin → Employees, add each person and give them a 4-digit PIN. That PIN is how they clock in and out.

041 min

Share one link

In Settings → Share with Employees, copy the link and send it to everyone. They open it on their own phone and clock in — no accounts needed.
