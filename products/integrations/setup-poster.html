⏱ Worktrail

Set up your kiosk in 4 simple steps

Employees clock in from any device — all synced through one link.

1

🔥 Create your free database

Go to firebase.google.com → Realtime Database → Create (test mode) → copy the URL that ends in firebaseio.com.

💡 Bought Done-for-you Hosting? Skip this — we do it for you.
2

⚙️ Set up Worktrail

Open the app → Set Up My Kiosk. Enter your business name, logo & color, choose an admin password, then paste your Firebase URL.

3

👥 Add your team

In Admin → Employees, add each person and give them a 4-digit PIN. That PIN is how they clock in and out.

4

🔗 Share one link

In Settings → Share with Employees, copy the link and send it to everyone. They open it on their own phone and clock in — no accounts needed.

One link → everyone in sync
