🪧 Signpost
Visitor Sign-In · Quick Setup Guide

Thanks for your purchase! Signpost is a single file that runs on any device — no install, no internet, no account. You'll be ready in about two minutes.

Set up your kiosk

1
Open the app.

Double-tap signpost.html on the device you'll use at the front desk (a tablet is ideal). It opens right in the browser.

2
Open Settings.

Tap ⚙ Staff (bottom-right), then enter the default passcode 1234 and open the Settings tab.

3
Brand it.

Add your business name, upload your logo, pick a brand color, and write a welcome message. Set the visit reasons your guests will pick from.

4
Choose your fields.

Toggle whether to ask for company, host, and phone/email — and whether a photo is required. Change the staff passcode from 1234 to your own. Tap Save settings.

5
Leave it on the welcome screen.

That's it — hand the device to visitors. They tap Sign In to arrive and Sign Out to leave. Times are recorded automatically.

Everyday use

›

See who's in the building: the on-site count shows on the welcome screen.

›

View the log / export: tap ⚙ Staff → enter passcode → Log tab. Search by name, tap CSV to download a spreadsheet, or Print for a paper copy.

Where is my data? Everything is stored privately in the browser on this one device — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Keep the device on and avoid "clear browsing data" for this page. Export a CSV now and then as your own backup. For a second entrance, run the file on a second device (each keeps its own log).
Tip — pin it full-screen. On iPad/Android, open signpost.html in the browser and choose Add to Home Screen for a clean, full-screen kiosk. Enable Guided Access (iPad) or Screen Pinning (Android) to lock guests to the app.
