Appointment Booking is a complete self-serve scheduler for your business, in a single file. You set it
up once: add your **services** (each with its own length and optional price), your **weekly hours**, a
**buffer** between appointments, a **minimum notice**, how far ahead people can book, and any **blocked
dates** for holidays or vacation.

Then clients just: **pick a service → pick a day with openings → pick an open time → enter their details
→ done.** Time slots are generated live from your rules, and the moment a time is booked it disappears —
so your schedule stays clean. You manage everything from a built-in **Appointments** list.

**Use it three ways:**
- **At the front desk / on any device** — book walk-ins and phone calls yourself; appointments save privately on that device. Open **Appointments** to search, cancel (which reopens the slot), export **CSV**, or print.
- **As a link you post** — host the file (free), then share the link on your site, Google profile, or email signature. Add `?embed=1` for a clean, client-only view.
- **Embedded in your website** — drop in one `<iframe>` (Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, Webflow, GoDaddy, etc.).

**Get bookings delivered:** paste a free delivery URL (Web3Forms/Formspree for **email**, a Google Sheet,
or a **Zapier/Make** webhook into your calendar, CRM, or SMS) and every booking is sent to you
automatically. This is how you collect bookings from a **posted or embedded** page. A step-by-step guide
is included.

**Who it's for:** salons, barbers, tutors, trainers, coaches, consultants, therapists, cleaners, pet
groomers, photographers, and any solo pro or small business that wants online booking without paying a
monthly scheduling-app subscription.

**What you get:**
- **Services** with duration + optional price (add as many as you offer)
- **Weekly hours** per day, **buffer** between appointments, **minimum notice**, and a **booking window**
- **Blocked dates** for days off, holidays, and vacation
- **Live availability** — slots built from your rules; booked times disappear (no double-booking on your schedule)
- Clean **4-step booking flow** with a confirmation reference
- **Appointments** list with search, cancel-to-reopen, **CSV export**, and print
- **Day / Night** (light & dark) mode
- Brand it: **business name, heading, intro text, and logo**
- **Website integration:** post as a link, `?embed=1` clean mode, one-line iframe embed
- **Optional delivery** of bookings to email / spreadsheet / CRM via any webhook

**Why buy it:**
- **Pay once.** No subscriptions, no per-booking fees, ever.
- **You own your data.** On-device by default; add a delivery URL only when you want bookings emailed.
- **Set up in minutes.** Add services & hours, then post the link or embed it. Guide included.
- **Flexible rules** — buffers, notice, windows, and blocked dates give you real control.
