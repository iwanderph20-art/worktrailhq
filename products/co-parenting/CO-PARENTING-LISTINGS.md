Add both parents (with their own colors) and each child, then **auto-fill your custody rotation** in
seconds. The top of the screen always answers the one question that matters — **whose day is it?** —
and every day is color-coded so there's never a mix-up.

Log **handoffs, activities, school events, doctor visits, and shared expenses**, each with a time,
location, the responsible parent, the child it's for, and a note. Flip to the **Agenda** view for a
clean run-down of what's coming up. It's calm, neutral, and built to lower the friction between two homes.

**Custody rotations, done for you:**
Pick a pattern — **week on / week off, 2-2-3, 2-2-5-5, 3-4-4-3, or every-other-weekend** — set a start
date and who starts, and the whole calendar fills in. Adjust any single day with a tap.

- **On your device** — everything saves privately on your phone or laptop; nothing is uploaded. Add it to your home screen for a one-tap calendar.
- **As a link you share** — host the file free, then send a link. Add `?embed=1` for a clean, read-only view for a grandparent, sitter, or older child.
- **Keep the co-parent in sync** — paste a free delivery URL and every new event is emailed or logged to a shared sheet automatically — no account for them to create.
