Whose day is it?

The top of the calendar always shows today's parent and how many events are on. Each day is color-coded by parent, so there's never a mix-up about who has the kids.

🔁

Auto custody rotations

Pick a pattern — week on / week off, 2-2-3, 2-2-5-5, 3-4-4-3, or every-other-weekend — set a start date and who starts, and the whole calendar fills in. Adjust any single day with a tap.

🧒

Your kids, color-tagged

Add each child with their own color. Every event can be tagged to one or more kids, so activities and appointments are easy to tell apart at a glance.

🔄

Handoffs & activities

Log pickups, drop-offs, practices, lessons, school events, and doctor visits — with times, locations, who's responsible, and notes for the other parent.

💵

Shared-expense notes

Record a cost on any event — coats, camp, co-pays — with a note on how it splits. Everything exports to a CSV for your records or mediator.
