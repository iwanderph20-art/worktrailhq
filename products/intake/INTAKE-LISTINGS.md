**Description:**
Client Intake is a complete, professional intake form for a law practice in a single file. A
prospective client enters their contact details, picks a **practice area** — Litigation, Estate
Planning, Elder Law, Guardianship, Probate, or Power of Attorney / Advance Directives — and a
smart second menu narrows to **exactly what they need** (e.g. *Adult guardianship*, *Contest a
will*, *Medicaid planning*, *Personal injury*). They describe the matter, note urgency and any
court deadline, list other parties (for your **conflict check**), and choose the **best way and
time to reach them**. On submit they see a clear confirmation that their inquiry was received and
someone will contact them shortly.

**Get inquiries delivered:** paste a free delivery URL (Web3Forms/Formspree for **email**, a Google
Sheet, or a **Zapier/Make** webhook into Clio, MyCase, Slack, SMS…) and every submission is sent to
you automatically. A step-by-step guide is included.

**Who it's for:** solo attorneys and small law firms handling litigation, estate & elder law,
